Instructions to the Surveyor:

Question to be asked on completion of the survey -

Do you know any person with disability / transgender person living in your neighbourhood?

  • If yes, ask where they live and if you can get introduced to that family to do the survey. Do the survey in that household.
  • If no, move on to the next house according to the sampling procedure guidelines.

If you come across any child in distress:

  • If the family seems willing to seek help, guide the family to the child helpline number (1098) or to the YUVA centre project incharge
  • If the family is not welcoming any external interest or help, report to the YUVA centre project incharge.
